# Separate real catalog from scale-test data

NosePrint will keep a provenance-checked Real Catalog separate from a generated Scale-Test Catalog. The initial Real Catalog may be seeded from the 2,191-row CC0 Perfume Recommendation Dataset only after a provenance audit, while 10,000 clearly synthetic Fragrance Editions will exercise ANN scale without appearing in shopping results. United States price snapshots will be curated separately for an initial subset; a larger public shopping catalog requires permission or a commercially licensed source whose license chain has been reviewed.
